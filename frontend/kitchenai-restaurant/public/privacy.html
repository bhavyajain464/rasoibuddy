Privacy Policy

Rasoi Buddy Partner · Package: com.kitchenai.restaurant · Last updated: June 18, 2026

Rasoi Buddy Partner (“we”, “our”, “the app”) is the restaurant staff application for Rasoi Buddy. It helps outlet owners and staff manage menu, stock, procurement, orders, and partner integrations. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google, as allowed by your Google account settings.
  • Outlet & restaurant data — Outlet name, menu items, recipes and ingredients, inventory and stock levels, shopping/procurement lists, orders, and related operational data you enter or sync in the app.
  • Team & access data — Staff email addresses, roles (owner, manager, staff), outlet IDs, and invite codes used to add team members to an outlet.
  • Partner integration data — If you connect a delivery partner such as Zomato, session credentials you provide (for example, browser cookies after you sign in through the in-app flow) are sent to our backend so we can sync menus and orders on your behalf. We store only what is needed to keep the integration running.
  • Device & technical data — Basic device information, app version, and API request metadata (such as IP address and timestamps) needed to operate and secure the service.

2. How we use information

  • Provide and improve restaurant features (menu, stock, buy lists, orders, team management).
  • Authenticate you, associate you with the correct outlet, and enforce role-based access.
  • Sync data with connected partner platforms when you start an integration worker.
  • Run optional AI-assisted features (for example, suggesting recipe ingredients) using our backend and configured AI providers.
  • Comply with law and prevent abuse.

3. Third-party services

We use trusted providers that process data on our behalf, including:

  • Google — Sign-in (Google OAuth).
  • Cloud hosting & database — Application backend and data storage (for example, Google Cloud).
  • Delivery partners — Such as Zomato, when you choose to connect an account and enable sync.
  • AI providers — To power optional suggestions (for example, Groq or Google Gemini, depending on configuration).
  • Expo / EAS — App builds and delivery infrastructure.

These providers have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers listed above, with delivery partners when you explicitly connect and run a sync, between staff members of the same outlet according to their roles, or when required by law.

5. Data retention & deletion

We retain your data while your account and outlet access are active and as needed to provide the service. Outlet owners can remove staff access. You may sign out at any time. To request account or data deletion, contact us at the email below.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures such as HTTPS for API communication and secure session tokens. Partner session credentials are stored on our servers only to operate integrations you enable. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your responsibilities

If you are an outlet owner or manager, you are responsible for inviting only trusted staff and for ensuring that partner account credentials you connect belong to your restaurant and are used in line with the partner platform’s terms.

8. Children

Rasoi Buddy Partner is a business application and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy at this URL and update the “Last updated” date.
