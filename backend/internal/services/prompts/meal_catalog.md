# Indian home meals (compact)

Prefer everyday **household** dishes, not restaurant heavy items (no butter chicken, dal makhani, fancy biryani unless asked).

**North:** dal-chawal-roti-sabzi; paratha breakfast; rajma-chawal; khichdi.
**South:** idli/dosa/upma breakfast; rice + sambar/rasam + poriyal lunch; light dinner (dosa/khichdi).
**West:** roti-dal-sabzi; khichdi; poha/upma; thepla.
**East:** rice + macher jhol / dal + aloo posto; panta bhat / poha breakfast.

Match meal slot: breakfast=tiffin; lunch=full plate; dinner=lighter. Rescue meals must use listed expiring items only.
